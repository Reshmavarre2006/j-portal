Job-Portal-Application

A job portal is a web-based platform that connects employers (companies) with job seekers. It allows companies to post job openings and allows users (candidates) to search, apply for, and sometimes track their applications. Job portals streamline the recruitment process by offering various tools for both employers and job seekers.
